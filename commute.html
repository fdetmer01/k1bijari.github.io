Commute between Ceremony and Reception Locations


The ceremony will take place at Faith Lutheran Church located at 311 Broadway in Cambridge, MA. The reception will be at Bellevue Golf Club, 320 Porter St, Melrose, MA.

Faith Lutheran Church, Cambridge, MA.
Bellevue Golf Club, Melrose, MA.

Between those two locations, there is a ~ 25-minute drive via I-93 N and US-1 N. The route includes tolls on US-1 N ($1.25 as of 4/2021) and it is the easiest if you have an EZPass. (For those of you getting a rental car and planning to explore the area during an extended stay, getting an EZPass might generally be a good idea; there are several places where it can be purchased, for more information visit mass.gov/ezdrivema .)
